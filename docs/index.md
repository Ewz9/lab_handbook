### Lab Culture & Values

#### 1. Professionalism & Rigor
<div style="padding:16px; background-color:#f8f9fa; border-radius:8px; border:1px solid #e9ecef; margin-bottom:20px;">
<p>
We expect all members to develop solid technical skills and maintain rigorous academic standards.<br>
Our goal is not short-term results, but sustainable competence in AI.
</p>
</div>

#### 2. Research Integrity
<div style="padding:16px; background-color:#f8f9fa; border-radius:8px; border:1px solid #e9ecef; margin-bottom:20px;">
<p>All research must adhere to the following principles:</p>

<ul>
  <li><strong>Reliability</strong>: Data must be accurate and truthful</li>
  <li><strong>Reproducibility</strong>: Experiments must be verifiable</li>
  <li><strong>Critical Thinking</strong>: Do not blindly trust AI-generated outputs</li>
</ul>
</div>

#### 3. Initiative & Responsibility
<div style="padding:16px; background-color:#f8f9fa; border-radius:8px; border:1px solid #e9ecef; margin-bottom:20px;">
<p>Research progress is the responsibility of each individual.</p>

<p><strong>Expected behaviors:</strong></p>
<ol>
  <li>Read relevant literature</li>
  <li>Implement and test methods</li>
  <li>Identify problems and propose solutions</li>
</ol>
</div>

#### 4. Resource & Environment
<div style="padding:16px; background-color:#f8f9fa; border-radius:8px; border:1px solid #e9ecef; margin-bottom:20px;">
<ul>
  <li>Keep the environment clean</li>
  <li>Use equipment properly</li>
  <li>Avoid misuse or damage</li>
</ul>
</div>

<p>Maintain a safe and sustainable research environment for all members.</p>

#### 1. 專業能力與嚴謹態度
<div style="padding:16px; background-color:#f8f9fa; border-radius:8px; border:1px solid #e9ecef; margin-bottom:20px;">
<p>
本實驗室期望每位成員培養紮實的專業能力與嚴謹的學術態度，強調可持續累積的核心能力。
</p>
</div>

#### 2. 學術誠信與研究品質

#### 4. 資源維護與實驗室環境
