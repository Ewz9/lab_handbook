### Lab Culture & Values

#### 1. Professionalism & Rigor
<div style="padding:12px 14px; background-color:#f8f9fa; border-radius:6px; border:1px solid #e9ecef; margin-bottom:12px;">
<p style="margin:0;">
We expect all members to develop strong technical skills and maintain rigorous academic standards.<br>
Our goal is not short-term results, but the development of sustainable and transferable expertise in AI.
</p>
</div>

#### 2. Research Integrity
<div style="padding:12px 14px; background-color:#f8f9fa; border-radius:6px; border:1px solid #e9ecef; margin-bottom:12px;">
<p style="margin:0 0 6px 0;">All research must adhere to the following principles:</p>

<ul style="margin:0;">
  <li><strong>Reliability</strong>: Data must be accurate and truthful</li>
  <li><strong>Reproducibility</strong>: Experiments must be verifiable and repeatable</li>
  <li><strong>Critical Thinking</strong>: Do not rely on unverified AI-generated outputs</li>
</ul>
</div>

#### 3. Initiative & Responsibility
<div style="padding:12px 14px; background-color:#f8f9fa; border-radius:6px; border:1px solid #e9ecef; margin-bottom:12px;">
<p style="margin:0 0 6px 0;">
Research progress is the responsibility of each individual. Members are expected to take initiative in their work.
</p>

<p style="margin:0;"><strong>Expected behaviors:</strong></p>
<ol style="margin:4px 0 0 16px;">
  <li>Read relevant literature</li>
  <li>Implement and validate methods</li>
  <li>Identify problems and propose solutions</li>
</ol>
</div>

#### 4. Resource & Environment
<div style="padding:12px 14px; background-color:#f8f9fa; border-radius:6px; border:1px solid #e9ecef; margin-bottom:12px;">
<ul style="margin:0;">
  <li>Maintain a clean working environment</li>
  <li>Use equipment properly</li>
  <li>Avoid misuse or unnecessary resource consumption</li>
</ul>
</div>

<p style="margin:8px 0;">Maintain a safe and sustainable research environment for all members.</p>

#### 5. Respect & Accountability
<div style="padding:12px 14px; background-color:#f8f9fa; border-radius:6px; border:1px solid #e9ecef; margin-bottom:12px;">
<p style="margin:0 0 6px 0;">
Mutual respect and accountability are fundamental to a professional research environment.
</p>

<ul style="margin:0;">
  <li>Maintain respectful communication with advisors and lab members</li>
  <li>Respond to messages and meeting requests in a timely manner</li>
  <li>Do not become unresponsive or unavailable without prior notice</li>
  <li>Take responsibility for your commitments and deadlines</li>
</ul>
</div>

### 實驗室風氣與價值觀

#### 1. 專業能力與嚴謹態度
<div style="padding:12px 14px; background-color:#f8f9fa; border-radius:6px; border:1px solid #e9ecef; margin-bottom:12px;">
<p style="margin:0;">
本實驗室期望每位成員培養紮實的專業能力與嚴謹的學術態度，強調可持續累積且可轉移的核心能力。
</p>
</div>

#### 2. 學術誠信與研究品質
<div style="padding:12px 14px; background-color:#f8f9fa; border-radius:6px; border:1px solid #e9ecef; margin-bottom:12px;">
<ul style="margin:0;">
  <li><strong>真實可靠</strong>：實驗數據須真實</li>
  <li><strong>可驗證性</strong>：研究過程須可重現</li>
  <li><strong>批判思考</strong>：不得盲信未驗證之 AI 結果</li>
</ul>
</div>

#### 3. 自主學習與責任
<div style="padding:12px 14px; background-color:#f8f9fa; border-radius:6px; border:1px solid #e9ecef; margin-bottom:12px;">
<p style="margin:0;"><strong>建議基本行為：</strong></p>
<ol style="margin:4px 0 0 16px;">
  <li>主動閱讀文獻</li>
  <li>實作與驗證方法</li>
  <li>整理問題並提出解法</li>
</ol>
</div>

#### 4. 資源維護與實驗室環境
<div style="padding:12px 14px; background-color:#f8f9fa; border-radius:6px; border:1px solid #e9ecef; margin-bottom:12px;">
<ul style="margin:0;">
  <li>維持環境整潔</li>
  <li>正確使用設備</li>
  <li>避免不當使用或資源浪費</li>
</ul>
</div>

<p style="margin:8px 0;">共同維護安全且可持續的研究環境。</p>

#### 5. 尊重與守信
<div style="padding:12px 14px; background-color:#f8f9fa; border-radius:6px; border:1px solid #e9ecef; margin-bottom:12px;">
<p style="margin:0 0 6px 0;">
良好的研究環境建立於彼此尊重與基本的責任感之上。
</p>

<ul style="margin:0;">
  <li>與指導教授及實驗室成員保持基本禮貌與尊重</li>
  <li>應適時回覆訊息與會議安排</li>
  <li>不得無故失聯或長時間未回應</li>
  <li>對自身承諾之工作與時程負責</li>
</ul>
</div>
