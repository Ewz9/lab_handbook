### Lab Culture & Values

#### 1. Professionalism & Rigor
<div style="padding:12px 16px; background-color:#f8f9fa; border-radius:8px; border:1px solid #e9ecef; margin-top:5px; margin-bottom:10px;">
We expect all members to develop solid technical skills and maintain rigorous academic standards. Our goal is not short-term results, but sustainable competence in AI.
</div>

#### 2. Research Integrity
<div style="padding:12px 16px; background-color:#f8f9fa; border-radius:8px; border:1px solid #e9ecef; margin-top:5px; margin-bottom:10px;">
<strong>Principles:</strong> 
• <strong>Reliability</strong>: Accurate data • <strong>Reproducibility</strong>: Verifiable results • <strong>Critical Thinking</strong>: Verify AI outputs.
</div>

#### 3. Initiative & Responsibility
<div style="padding:12px 16px; background-color:#f8f9fa; border-radius:8px; border:1px solid #e9ecef; margin-top:5px; margin-bottom:10px;">
Research progress is an individual responsibility. Members should:
1. Read literature 2. Implement methods 3. Propose solutions.
</div>

#### 4. Resource & Environment
<div style="padding:12px 16px; background-color:#f8f9fa; border-radius:8px; border:1px solid #e9ecef; margin-top:5px; margin-bottom:10px;">
Keep the environment clean, use equipment properly, and avoid misuse to ensure a sustainable research space.
</div>

#### 5. Respect & Accountability
<div style="padding:12px 16px; background-color:#f8f9fa; border-radius:8px; border:1px solid #e9ecef; margin-top:5px; margin-bottom:10px;">
Maintain respectful communication, respond to messages timely, and take full responsibility for your commitments and deadlines.
</div>

### 實驗室風氣與價值觀

#### 1. 專業能力與嚴謹態度
<div style="padding:12px 16px; background-color:#f8f9fa; border-radius:8px; border:1px solid #e9ecef; margin-top:5px; margin-bottom:10px;">
本實驗室期望每位成員培養紮實的專業能力與嚴謹的學術態度，強調可持續累積的核心能力。
</div>

#### 2. 學術誠信與研究品質
<div style="padding:12px 16px; background-color:#f8f9fa; border-radius:8px; border:1px solid #e9ecef; margin-top:5px; margin-bottom:10px;">
• <strong>真實可靠</strong>：數據須真實 • <strong>可驗證性</strong>：過程須可重現 • <strong>批判思考</strong>：不得盲信 AI 結果。
</div>

#### 3. 自主學習與責任
<div style="padding:12px 16px; background-color:#f8f9fa; border-radius:8px; border:1px solid #e9ecef; margin-top:5px; margin-bottom:10px;">
<strong>核心行為：</strong> 主動閱讀文獻、實作驗證方法、整理問題並提出解法。
</div>

#### 4. 資源維護與實驗室環境
<div style="padding:12px 16px; background-color:#f8f9fa; border-radius:8px; border:1px solid #e9ecef; margin-top:5px; margin-bottom:10px;">
維持環境整潔、正確使用設備、避免不當使用，共同維護安全且可持續的研究環境。
</div>

#### 5. 尊重與守信
<div style="padding:12px 16px; background-color:#f8f9fa; border-radius:8px; border:1px solid #e9ecef; margin-top:5px; margin-bottom:10px;">
保持禮貌、準時回覆訊息與會議、不無故失聯，並對自身承諾的工作與時程負責。
</div>
